# User Guide

Welcome to Apex AI! This guide will help you get started with the app and use its features to achieve your fitness goals.

## Getting Started

1.  **Download the app:** Apex AI is available on both iOS and Android. Download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.
2.  **Create an account:** Open the app and create a new account using your email and password.
3.  **Set up your profile:** After creating your account, you will be prompted to set up your profile. Enter your name, weight, height, and fitness goals. This information will help the AI coach provide you with personalized recommendations.

## Main Features

### Home

The **Home** screen is your dashboard. It provides an overview of your daily progress, including your calorie and water intake, your workout streak, and AI-powered suggestions for your next workout.

### Train

The **Train** screen is where you can manage your workouts.

*   **Create a workout:** You can create your own custom workout routines by adding exercises, sets, and reps.
*   **Start a workout:** To start a workout, simply select it from your list of workouts and tap the "Start" button.
*   **Track your workout:** During your workout, you can log your sets, reps, and weight for each exercise.

### Fuel

The **Fuel** screen is where you can track your nutrition.

*   **Log your meals:** You can log your meals by entering the food items and their nutritional information.
*   **Log your water intake:** You can also log your daily water intake to stay hydrated.

### Photos

The **Photos** screen allows you to track your progress visually.

*   **Add progress photos:** You can upload and store progress photos to see how your body is changing over time.

### Stats

The **Stats** screen provides detailed reports and charts on your workout and nutrition data.

*   **Track your progress:** You can track your body weight, workout volume, and other metrics over time.
*   **Visualize your data:** The app provides charts and graphs to help you visualize your progress.

### Coach

The **Coach** screen provides AI-powered suggestions and feedback.

*   **Daily suggestions:** The AI coach will provide you with daily workout suggestions based on your goals and recent activities.
*   **Personalized feedback:** The coach will also provide you with personalized feedback and encouragement to help you stay motivated.
