# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the product requirements for the Apex AI mobile application. It covers the key features, user stories, and technical specifications for the app.

## 2. Goals

The primary goals of Apex AI are to:

*   Provide users with a simple and intuitive way to track their fitness activities.
*   Help users stay motivated and engaged in their fitness journey.
*   Leverage AI to provide personalized recommendations and insights.

## 3. User Personas

### 3.1. The Beginner

*   **Name:** Alex
*   **Age:** 25
*   **Occupation:** Software Developer
*   **Goals:** Get in shape, learn how to work out effectively.
*   **Frustrations:** Feels intimidated by the gym, doesn't know where to start.

### 3.2. The Experienced Lifter

*   **Name:** Sarah
*   **Age:** 32
*   **Occupation:** Graphic Designer
*   **Goals:** Build muscle, increase strength.
*   **Frustrations:** Hits plateaus in her training, needs help with programming.

## 4. Features

### 4.1. User Authentication

*   Users can sign up for a new account using their email and password.
*   Users can sign in to their existing account.
*   Users can sign out of their account.

### 4.2. Profile Setup

*   Users can set up their profile with their name, weight, height, and fitness goals.
*   Users can update their profile information at any time.

### 4.3. Workout Tracking

*   Users can create and manage their own workout routines.
*   Users can track their workouts in real-time, including exercises, sets, reps, and weight.
*   Users can view their workout history and progress over time.

### 4.4. Nutrition Tracking

*   Users can log their daily meals and water intake.
*   Users can set calorie and water intake goals.
*   Users can view their nutrition history and progress over time.

### 4.5. AI Coach

*   The AI coach provides daily workout suggestions based on the user's goals and recent activities.
*   The AI coach provides feedback and encouragement to help users stay motivated.

### 4.6. Progress Photos

*   Users can upload and store progress photos to visually track their transformation.

### 4.7. Reports and Stats

*   Users can view detailed reports and stats on their workout and nutrition data.
*   Users can track their body weight and other body measurements over time.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

*   **Performance:** The app should be fast and responsive, with a smooth user experience.
*   **Security:** User data should be stored securely and protected from unauthorized access.
*   **Usability:** The app should be easy to use and navigate, even for users who are not tech-savvy.
*   **Reliability:** The app should be reliable and available when users need it.
