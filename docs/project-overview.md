# Project Overview

## Introduction

Apex AI is a mobile application designed to help users achieve their fitness goals through personalized workout and nutrition tracking. The app leverages AI to provide daily suggestions and helps users stay motivated by tracking their progress over time.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of Apex AI is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for fitness enthusiasts to:

*   Track their workouts, including exercises, sets, reps, and weight.
*   Log their daily nutrition and water intake.
*   Monitor their body weight and other body measurements.
*   Visualize their progress through charts and reports.
*   Receive AI-powered suggestions for workouts and nutrition.

## Target Audience

The target audience for Apex AI includes:

*   Gym-goers and fitness enthusiasts who want to track their progress.
*   Individuals who are new to fitness and need guidance on workouts and nutrition.
*   People who want to improve their overall health and well-being.
